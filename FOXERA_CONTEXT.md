## 3. SCORECARD (BOT DÙNG KHI ĐỀ XUẤT NICHE/IDEA)
- **Niche Score /40** = Demand + Personalization + Visual Depth + Collection Depth + Low IP Risk + Product Fit + Gift Fit + Seasonal Extendability (mỗi tiêu chí 1–5). **Chỉ đề xuất scale khi ≥30/40.**
- **Idea Score /50** (10 tiêu chí ×5: demand, differentiation, embroidery fit, thumbnail, personalization, collection, margin, policy, evergreen, brand fit). **≥38 triển khai · 32–37 test nhỏ · <32 bỏ.**
- Mỗi concept phải có **≥2 selling driver** (vd: artwork+personalization, profession+occasion, location+souvenir, mascot+humor).
