## Evidence labels

- `INTERNAL_LOCKED`: confirmed internal rule or decision.
- `INTERNAL_OBSERVED`: found in internal project files but not necessarily permanent.
- `INTERNAL_HYPOTHESIS`: modeled persona, forecast, proposal, or unverified claim.
- `OBSERVED_CURRENT`: observed on an official live site on 2026-07-20.
- `INFERRED`: strategic interpretation from evidence.
- `STALE_RISK`: must be checked again before execution.
- `UNVERIFIED`: must not be repeated as fact.

## Core rule

Never merge evidence and speculation. Every factual record must include source, observation date, evidence label, confidence, and freshness requirement.

## Strategic headline

GritFell should win as an **accessible-premium outdoor lifestyle identity brand**: specific pursuit culture, restrained heritage art, gift-buyer insight, approachable pricing, and collection storytelling. It should not try to out-technical SITKA, AFTCO, Huk, or Duck Camp.
